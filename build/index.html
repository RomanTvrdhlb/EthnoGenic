• enthogenica

    Be Human in Kind

    conscious empathic reflective aware

  • A breath, a sense, a sound, a sight. What
    is there to improve or disapprove of?..

  • I am sexual and, thus, egoic...
    I am sensory-perceptual and, thus, erotic...
    I am alive and, thus, empathic...
    I am interactive and, thus, entheogenic...

    This is what I am, not who I am, by being human in kind. The who of “I am” is but a sounded breath of speech and an echo in the void...

  • To have an ego and to be sexual are as distinct as being a person and having sex. Any person who has a belly bottom also has an ego... Instinctual, emotional and intellectual, the ego is “the organizing mental apparatus that is the sum of the integrated central nervous system”... To deny, despise or decry having an ego is to battle with the ego and, as such, be in conflict with what is: the given condition of being human in kind...

  • As the expression of desire, I am sensory-perceptual. Being moved by desire, I am erotic... Interdependent rather than monogamous, to relate to the erotic as a pronoun or the locked property of me, my, mine, causes a healthy cell to turn cancerous. Doing so is analogous with defending peace through warfare...

  • What causes I am to be alive is none other than relationship. The bond of all relating is empathy — not blood and bones, or egg and sperm, or ashes and dust... Empathy is the essence of connection. Relationship is made of empathy, just as the essence of aliveness is relationship... Connection is made where empathy is — not by relating for the sake of sex, or to outrun death, or to realize the pure land of nirvana...

  • I interact because I am an integral fractal, fulcrum and fountainhead of consciousness, without being sealed up within myself... Any interaction reaches across the gap between outward and inward and, as such, is equally exogenic and endogenic—in other words, entheogenic... Interaction is surrendering to the entheogenic nature of I am. By embracing the given conditions of what and who I am, I interact...

  • To face and embrace oneself in the here and now is psychoactive, not celestial nor terrestrial. It is sonic, not light nor dark; it is affirmative, not positive nor negative; it is interdependent, not predetermined nor random... To face and embrace oneself in the here and now is what enthogenica does up close and personal...

  • No one can embrace their given condition while identifying with a dismal and debilitating perspective of oneself, like a sleepwalker lost in a dream or a drunkard who mistakes intoxication for being present...

    Shedding inherited stereotypes, shames and safeguards happens in the very moment they are confronted — not by judging or making fun of them...

  • Enthogenica is not a how-to self-improvement, it does not absolve the problems with the same kind of thinking through which they are generated... Rather, enthogenica takes to the ingestion of psychoactive catalysts to bring that which is blended and blotted out into awareness... Thus, the continuous emergence from inherited stereotypes, shames and safeguards comes about...

  • Linguistically, psychoactive catalysts and psychedelics are both neologisms — mind altering connotations. They activate arousal that brings to the fore what is present from origination: aliveness...

    Contact with anything is psychoactive, just as everything is a catalyst of consciousness: the corpus, consonant and cosmos of phenomena...

  • Given rather than taken, psychoactive catalysts reflect that which heralds insight and embrace: being present...

    When taken on one’s own, psychoactive catalysts merely fan the flames of imagination, infatuation and idolization...

  • Being human in kind is beheld and witnessed in relationship. The same holds true for the ingestion of psychoactive catalysts...

    I am, you are, egoic as sex...
    I am, you are, erotic as sensory-perception...
    I am, you are, empathic as aliveness...
    I am, you are, entheogenic as the intercourse of relationship...
    I am, you are, selfless as love and unscripted as stillness...
    Neither you nor I are greater or lesser than, united with or divided from the integral hologram of coexistence: consciousness...

    When kindness is lost the egoic turns into possessiveness, the erotic into power, the empathic into profit and the entheogenic into prestige. Then compassion — the root of kindness — cannot blossom on the horizon of consciousness...

    When kindness is lost, being human is freeze framed in imagination and vacuum sealed in the postdated mausoleum of confabulated memory...

    Hence the hopeless, hounded and humbled are trampled over in pursuit of reaching some higher ground or realizing some deeper cause than what anyone already is by being human in kind: conscious, empathic, reflective, aware...

    What is beheld here, as well as the one beholding, is precious. To reflect upon what is seen and to listen into what is heard is the sine qua non of being human in the here and now: kindness...

    • To be aware denotes none other than surrendering to what matters: clarity...
